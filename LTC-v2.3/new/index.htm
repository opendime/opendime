USB

Your Opendime™ is new and unused.
Follow these steps to pick a private key.

  1. By using Opendime you agree to our Terms of Sale and Use.
  2. Open your File Explorer and look for OPENDIME drive (on MacOS /Volumes/OPENDIME).
  3. Copy files onto OPENDIME until at least of 256K bytes have been saved.
  4. Once that total is reached you will see the light blinking quickly, and the device will eject by itself. Ignore the system warning.
  5. Reload this page to see your new Litecoin address!
  6. If you end up here again, just add more files onto this drive.

Notes

  • Doesn't matter what the files are. They won't be saved.
  • One or two photos is usually enough.
  • All the randomness from those files goes into the random number generator.
  • Get random bits from random.org
  • Advanced users can copy from /dev/urandom
